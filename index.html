Introduction

Nebulopresents a rethinking of image generator designs. The software is offline, open source, and free, while at the same time, similar to many online image generators like Midjourney, the manual tweaking is not needed, and users only need to focus on the prompts and images.

Offline Requirement

  • 4 GB VRAM
  • 16 GB RAM
  • Rayzen 5 3600
  • 20 GB of Disk Space

This requires using Microsoft’s Virtual Swap technique, which is automatically enabled by your Windows installation in most cases, so you often do not need to do anything about it.

Limited Long-Term Support (LTS)

The Nebuloproject, built entirely on the Stable Diffusion XL architecture, is now in a state of limited long-term support (LTS) with bug fixes only. As the existing functionalities are considered as nearly free of programmartic issues (Thanks to mashb1t's huge efforts), future updates will focus exclusively on addressing any bugs that may arise.
